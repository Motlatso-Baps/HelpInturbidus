8.3.1.2 Removal Confirmation messages

This confirmation message appears when the Delete button has been clicked. The system will prompt to either confirm (Delete) or deny (Cancel) the removal.

• By clicking the Cancel button, the removal will not be confirmed, and the confirmation message will no longer be displayed
